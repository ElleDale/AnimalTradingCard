Emperor Penguin

The Emperor Penguin is the tallest and heaviest of the penguin species.

  • Scientific Name: Aptenodytes forsteri
  • Average Height: 3.6 – 4.3 ft. (Adult)
  • Average Lifespan: 15 – 20 years
  • Habitat: Endemic to Antartica

Emperor penguins are capable of diving to depths of approximately 550 metres (1,800 feet) in search of food; they are the world’s deepest-diving birds. Though they have wings and are considered to be birds, they are flightless.
